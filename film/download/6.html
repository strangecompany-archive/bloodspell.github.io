DVD?

The DVD release of BloodSpell is now available. With four commentary tracks, hours of additional material, documentaries on the film's production, deleted scenes, subtitles in English and German and more.

The DVD is available in downloadable ISO format for burning in normal DVD burners:

  • NTSC Version for US, Canada and Japan
  • PAL Version for other countries
  • Inlay and disc label images for the DVDs

Sadly we are not able to offer a physical media version at this time. Sorry!
